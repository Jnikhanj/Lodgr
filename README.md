# Lodgr v5 — Tax Workspace

A local-first Safari PWA for tracking Australian tax deductions and estimating an individual tax outcome.

## v5 additions

- Minimal section-based Tax tab matching the Lodgr visual system
- Income and PAYG withholding breakdown
- Salary sacrifice / salary packaging settings
- Itemised other income
- Work claims, personal super deduction and other deductions
- Medicare levy estimate with exemption days and low-income reduction
- Private hospital cover and Medicare levy surcharge estimate
- 2025–26 marginal HELP/VSL/SSL repayment estimate
- Linked spouse profile and dependant settings
- Tax offsets and credits
- Advanced income-test amounts
- Full estimate breakdown

## Important

Lodgr is an estimate and record-keeping tool. It is not a substitute for the ATO calculation process or advice from a registered tax agent. Private health rebate adjustments, family Medicare reductions and special tax circumstances may require additional review.

## Deploy

Upload all files in this folder to the root of the GitHub Pages repository. If an older installed PWA is cached, fully close and reopen Lodgr after the deployment. If required, remove and re-add the Home Screen app once.
